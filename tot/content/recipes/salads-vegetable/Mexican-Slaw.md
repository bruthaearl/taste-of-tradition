- 1 med. head cabbage, chopped
- 1 med. onion, chopped
- 1 bell pepper chopped

Mix together:
- 1 c. vinegar
- 3/4 c. sugar
- 1 t. salt
- 1 t. mustard seed
- 1 t. celery seed
- 1 t. tumeric

Boil the above seasonings together 5 minutes.
Pour over cabbage, onion and green pepper.
Placed in covered jar. Refrigerate over night.
