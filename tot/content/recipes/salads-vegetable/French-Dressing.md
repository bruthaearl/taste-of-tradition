- 1/2 c. sugar
- 1 t. salt
- 1 t. celery seed
- 1 t. paprika
- 1 t. dry mustard
- 1 1/2 t. finely chopped onion
- 1/4 c. vinegar
- 1 c. salad oil

Combine all ingredients in blender or mixer one minute. Stop. Scrape sides.
Blend again until thick. Refrigerate. If separates blend 1 min.
Served in small pitcher to be poured over salad.
