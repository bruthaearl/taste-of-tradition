- 8oz. cream cheese
- 4oz. Swiss cheese grated
- 4oz. Bleu cheese
- 1/2 t. Tobasco
- 1/4 cup chopped pecans

Mix all the cheese with Tobasco. Blend Well. Roll into ball. Chill.
On day of the party, roll in pecans. Good!
