---

Combine:
- 2 c. raw cranberries ground
- 3 c. tiny marshmallows
- 3/4 c. sugar

Cover and chill overnight

Add:
- 2 c. diced pared apples
- 1/2 c. seedless green grapes
- 1/2 c. English walnuts
- 1/4 t. salt

Fold in 1 c. heavy cream, whipped.
